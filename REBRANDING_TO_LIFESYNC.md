# App Rebranding: Family Tips ‚Üí LifeSync

## Overview
The app has been successfully rebranded from "Family Tips" to "LifeSync" to better reflect its comprehensive personal and family management features.

## Why "LifeSync"?
The new name **LifeSync** represents:
- **Life**: All aspects of personal and family life management
- **Sync**: Synchronization and organization of finances, tasks, health, shopping, and more in one unified platform

This name better captures the app's extensive feature set beyond just "tips" for families.
